Ad Formulas from Creative Brief
These match the exact structure from the creative brief. Hook → Identity → Work → Close. Every ad 20-25 sec MAX. Cut every 2-3 seconds. Iron is the hero. Captions on everything.
Ad 1 — Testimonial (15-20 sec)
Customer soundbite is the hook. Juan wraps it.
HOOK→ Best customer soundbite OR H1, H2, H5
PROOF→ Customer clip + iron fence beauty shots (5-10s)
STACK→ S1 + S3 as text overlays on best footage
CTA→ C1 or C3 (Juan back on camera)
Ad 2 — Walkthrough (20-25 sec)
Juan to camera. B-roll of iron work intercut every 2-3 sec.
HOOK→ H1, H3, H4, H5, or H8 (3 sec, on cam)
IDENTITY→ I1 or I3 (5-7 sec, cut to welding)
WORK→ W1 or W2 + W3 (8-10 sec, build footage)
CLOSE→ C1 or C3 (3-5 sec, on cam)
Short Reel (15 sec)
Quick hit for Stories / Reels / fast scroll.
HOOK→ H2, H5, H8, or H10
IDENTITY→ I1 (shortened)
STACK→ S1 + S3 (overlays)
CTA→ C3
Spanish Version
Same formula, full Spanish. Month 2 expansion.

IDENTITY→ I5

CTA→ C5
Editing Rules (From Creative Brief)
Non-Negotiables
Break these and the ad underperforms.
Captions on every spoken word — 85% watch on mute
Frame 1 = motion or face talking. No slow intros. No logos.
Cut every 2-3 sec. If one shot sits for 4+ sec, you lose people.
Iron fences are the hero. Best footage = iron close-ups, welds, gates.
Raw over polished. Shaky is fine. Authentic converts. Corporate kills trust.
Export 1080x1080 (feed) + 1080x1920 (stories) for every video.
Required Overlays
Place naturally. Max 2 on screen at once.
FREE Same-Day Estimate — during offer section
1-Year Warranty — during offer section
Custom Iron & Ornamental — during offer section
Only 4-5 Spots This Month — closing moment
Se Habla Espanol — last 3 seconds
Houston, TX — first 5 seconds
